Event tracking example

When you click on this link, Google Analytics will track the event with custom parameters specified by the attribute "data-analytics-event"!

When you click on this call to action link, Google Analtyics will track the event with default parameters since none are specified.

When you click on a named anchor link, Google Analtyics will track it!

When you click on this regular link, nothing unusual will happen and everything will work as normal.
